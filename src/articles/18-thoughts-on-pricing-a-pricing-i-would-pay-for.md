How many views is your personal/product blog getting on a monthly basis?  
  
Is it in range of 1-5k, 5-10k or more?  
  
I’m trying to figure out the pricing for onesimpleblog.com  
  
Right now it’s at $20/year with a soft limit of 100k views and 1 GB storage.   
  
This is something I would pay at the views I’m getting.   
  
It’s definitely way less than many blogging platforms out there but definitely costlier than free platforms.   
  
I also see platforms like Carrd where they have a starting plan of 9/year and one of the reason I started using it.   
  
So it makes perfect sense for me to keep the pricing I would pay for.
