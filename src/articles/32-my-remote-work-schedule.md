I have been working remotely for more than 5 years now.  
  
My work schedule had changed throughout the years.   
Currently it’s split into three focused chunk of time.   
  
First one right after my morning walk. Second one usually after breakfast but before lunch.  
That would mean two session of 2-3 hours before lunch.  
  
Then I take some break, spend some time with kid, rest, sometimes a nap.  
  
Then comes the last session (2-3 hours) before wrapping up for the day.   
I try to wrap up my work and post an update in Basecamp before 6pm.  
  
I used to be a night owl but took some effort to change.   
Now it feels good, both mentally and physically.
