Sorry that's all I can share.  
  
(being a minimalist, you know... get it?)  
  
Joke aside, I have been having this urge to simplify lot of things in my life.   
  
I just don't want to think or worry about them.   
  
To give you an example, I have a car back in India.   
  
I'm here in Dubai.   
  
Shall I leave that car there? Sell it? Let my parent use it once in a while?  
  
They already have their own but still use it once in a while, mainly to keep it in good shape but they enjoy it too.  
  
These might seem a silly thing but it is a thing and I can't just ignore it.   
  
That's just one small example there are plenty other things - be it items we own or responsibilities we agreed to.   
  
So yes, I'll going to minimise the number of things I need to worry or even think about.
