Life sucks.   
Right from the start.   
May be right after childhood.   
Lucky if we happened to be around parents.   
Many born with disease or no parents around.   
  
There is nothing we can do about.   
What we can do is make others’ life less horrible.  
  
Make them laugh.   
Spend time with them.   
Make their life less horrible.   
Make them feel like it’s worth living.    
  
Find that someone and they’ll make us feel the same.   
  
Hopefully. Yes, hope. We need that.   
Even if it feels like we have nothing else.   
Someone else is doing the same and they need us.  
Until then, let’s continue to make others’ life better.  
  
See you later. Spread love.
