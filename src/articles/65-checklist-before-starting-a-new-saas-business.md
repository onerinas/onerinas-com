First of all, I should not be starting another one as my current focus is on [FeedbackFace](https://feedbackface.com). Learned the hard way not to work on multiple things as it results on zero progress across all projects.   
  
This is a checklist I made for myself and followed while deciding to work on FeedbackFace

-   B2B? (no b2c, as we already have one - hifive)
-   What's the min monthly we can charge? should be >= $49
    -   later we can introduce lower prices or free options
    -   but at start keep min 49 to see if anyone pays and solves a real pain point
-   Have competitors list
    -   so its not a new market and we know businesses are paying for such products
    -   also to understand what market we are serving, what industry we are in
-   Do some SEO keywords research to figure out if we have any opportunities there (not an expert on this and still learning)
