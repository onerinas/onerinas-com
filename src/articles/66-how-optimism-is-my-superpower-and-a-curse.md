I am optimistic and don't lose hope easily.   
  
I always tend to believe something miraculous happens and it will work at the end.   
  
Be it at finding a bug or a personal goal I have.  
  
Superpower since it helps as a programmer.  
  
Curse as it's difficult to accept defeat and move on.
