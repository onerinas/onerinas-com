I started working on the blogging platform [One Simple Blog](https://onesimpleblog.com/) thinking it should be simple to build a blogging platform. 

  

It’s a well established market and doesn’t have to educate customers on what a blogging platform is. 

  

Nothing is as simple as it seems.

  

There is lot of things involved in building an app. Even for a simple blogging app.  
  
So here I am figuring out all the Lego pieces that goes into building a great product and a sustainable business.    
  
Good luck to us!
