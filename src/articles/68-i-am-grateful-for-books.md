I used to be in the "read a book? I'll just read the summary" crowd.  
  
I am grateful that people spend time to write and publish their books.  
  
Even if a book conveys just one single point, I like how authors tell different stories to convey them.  
  
I started realizing that these anecdotes are not just for entertainment. They help us instill ideas deep in our subconscious.  
  
That way, we truly understand the ideas and can apply them in our daily lives.  
  
This is more and more important in this fast-paced world where we are constantly bombarded with TikTok, Reels, Shorts, and whatnot.
