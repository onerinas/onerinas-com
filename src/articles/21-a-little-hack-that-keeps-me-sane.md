I have this reminder "What are you going to regret?" set for every 3 months.  

![](/images/articles/21-a-little-hack-that-keeps-me-sane-1.png)

Screenshot of Reminder app in Mac

It's been sitting there for two days and I don't know what I'll regret.  
  
Which can be a good sign too. Makes me think about things I'm doing recently and what I would like to do differently.  
  
As I type, I realised that there is something.  
  
Earlier this year, I promised myself I'll focus on building onesimpleblog.com this year: [https://x.com/onerinas/status/1735967040710611132](https://x.com/onerinas/status/1735967040710611132)  

![](/images/articles/21-a-little-hack-that-keeps-me-sane-2.png)

https://x.com/onerinas/status/1735967040710611132

And yet, I have been tempted to work on [WhatsNew.co](https://whatsnew.co/) and [TIL.dev](https://til.dev/) little bit. (even pulled into a new product idea about how we ~consume~ enjoy content from internet)  
  
Something I'll regret is if I work on other products AND don't have atleast one paying customer this year for [One Simple Blog](https://onesimpleblog.com/)  
  
So there you go, that's something I'm going to regret if steer away from One Simple Blog.
