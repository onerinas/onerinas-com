I got A+ for all subject except the native language (A in Malayalam) in 10th.   
  
From there it was downward hill in terms of academic performance.   
  
At the same time, that’s when I got into the game of living the life 😄  
  
started caring less about others, social pressure, and started living the moment.   
  
I didn’t care if I fail at exams, and I failed.   
  
People were surprised to see a kid who got straight A+, fail.   
  
For me, it was a breath of fresh air.   
  
Then I needed money to buy things and not bother my parents.   
  
So that’s exactly what I did, I made money.   
  
Started by taking photocopies of study materials and sell it to my classmates.   
  
Then I barely finished 12th and started college.   
  
Same theme there but a tad wilder as I was away from home.   
  
I’ll stop here for now :D
