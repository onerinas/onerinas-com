I joke with friends that I don’t trust them nor anybody.   
  
but on a serious note,   
  
- why does trust matter?  
  
- what does trust mean?   
  
- do you trust yourself? if yes, what do you trust yourself with?  
  
- did you trust someone in life recently? if yes, did they ask you to trust them?  
  
so many questions   
  
that said, it comes from a place of love.   
  
I don’t hate people as my first instinct.   
  
My point is that world would be a much better place if we have expectations from ourselves than from others.   
  
Talking about expectations as trust involves expectations as I understand it.   
  
Thoughts?
