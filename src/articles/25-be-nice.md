Being nice to others is not a bug; it's a feature and a feature we all need to aspire to live by.  
  
That doesn't mean we can't be truthful anymore. That's the argument I often hear. There's a way to communicate what we need to without being rude.   
  
It's a skill to learn and something I'm aspiring to learn.
